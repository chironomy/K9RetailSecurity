Training

K9 Retail Secuirty, LLC provides top quality Retail Gaurd dogs to protect your your business from wannabe intruders. A protection dog, while obedient guardians, are also social and great with other dogs. Our protection dog training procedures ensure all of our dogs are completely trained in on-leash and off-leash obedience for total control. In addition to obedience training, these dogs are trained and socialized to live comfortably in your business and at home with your family.

We offer business protection dogs for sale, trained at various levels for your business needs. Included with any protection dog purchase is a course designed to get your dog comfortable with his new procedures. Delivery is available all over the Southeast. We come to your place of business and work with you and your staff to ensure that you are comfortable working and living with your new protection dog.

Care

Although all protection dogs are trained to be self sustaining they still need loving homes. With all protection dogs the more time they spend with people they are comfortable with the more they will care about your business and property. while they are more than capable of living at the business they will always prefer to have a home life as well.
